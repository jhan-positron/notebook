  (lane[127:125]=cmd_type SAL_CMD=1 / SAL_LO_PRI_CMD=6,

  payload bit; no sal_build_*_cmd() takes a tdest argument.

  EXEC and raises UncoveredCommandWord for general compute words — for
  full compute, drive SimHemisphere.submit_*/accept_sal_cmd directly.
